What is misinformation ?

In terms of the language structure of false information, false information on the Internet should refer to information that is inconsistent with the facts. Network false information can be divided into the following two categories: first, defamatory network false information. Second, terrorist network false information.

The causes of false information appears

  • Win click rate and cheat readers with false information
  • Pursue one-sided economic news without paying attention to the authenticity of news
  • Weak sense of responsibility and check-in of network self media
  • Severe negative consequences stemming from the behavior
  • The editors' professional quality is poor,don't have ability to identify the true and false news
